Storing A Solution
NOTE: You must have logged into a user account and have a solution on your active workbench.
1.) Single click the cabinet in your user account where you want to store the solution.
The cabinets within your account are denoted by a key next to a rectangular cabinet door.
2.) Single click the solution on the active workbench you wish to store in your account.
The outline of the flask will change from black to a light blue, and the name of the flask will become highlighted.
3.) On the action bar located on the left-hand side of the active workbench, single click the "Store Solution" button.
The solution will not disappear from your workbench, but you will have a copy of the solution in your account.
