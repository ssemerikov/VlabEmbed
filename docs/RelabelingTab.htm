Relabeling a Tab
Note: You may want to relabel your tabs when you are working on several different workbenches or have a problem description open.
1.) Right click on the tab you want to rename.
A pop-up menu will be displayed.

Mac Users: You can access the Relabel option by pressing the F2 key on your keyboard.
2.) Drag the pointer down and click on the Rename option
A Relabel Tab dialogue box will appear.
3.) Type the new label for the tab and click Ok.
The dialogue box will disappear and the tab will bear the new label.
