Obtaining Solution Information
1.) Click on the solution about which you want to obtain information.
The flask will become highlighted. The Solution Info panel to the right of the workbench will show the name, volume, concentratin, temperature, and pH of the selected solution.
Your instructor may disable some of the features in the Solution Info panel for certain assignments.
2.) In the Solution Info panel to the right of the workbench, click on the circle next to the state of species (aqueous, solid, gas) you wish to view.
The concentration viewer, located beneath the circles, will show the amounts of each element in the solution.
Note: At present, the Virtual Lab does not simulate gases.
