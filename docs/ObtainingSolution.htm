Obtaining a Solution
1.) In the Stockroom Explorer window, double click on the IrYdium Solutions cabinet to open it.
A list of cabinets containing solutions will be displayed. Double click on the cabinet from which you want to retrieve a solution.
2.) Double click the solution you wish to add to your workbench.
An Erlenmeyer flask containing 100 mL will appear on your workbench, labeled with the name of the solution.
