Removing Equipment
1.) Click on the equipment you want to remove.
The name of the glassware or tool will become highlighted.
2.) Press Delete or Backspace.
The equipment will disappear.

You can also right click on the object and select Remove from the pop-up menu.

Note: At present, there is no Undo function. To clear a work area without removing equipment, see Adding a Workbench.
