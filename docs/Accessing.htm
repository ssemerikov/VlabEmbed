Accessing the Virtual Lab
1.) Launch a Web browser application.
2.) Load the IrYdium website:
http://ir.chem.cmu.edu/irProject/applets/virtualLab/
You may receive a new URL from your professor.
3.) In the list located on the left-hand side of the window, single click the "Applet" option.
If the applet does not run initially, you may need to download additional components for your browser. Refer to the online instructions for platform specific procedures.
